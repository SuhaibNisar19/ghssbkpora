#2 Sabeela Ma'am

Head of our School

Principal Sabeela Ma'am is the esteemed leader shaping the core of our school's educational journey. With a rich tapestry of experience and a visionary approach, she fosters academic excellence and holistic student development. Her leadership is marked by a commitment to innovation, critical thinking, and a profound respect for knowledge.

Known for her wise guidance, Sabeela Ma'am plays a pivotal role in steering our educational landscape. Her strategic vision ensures our school remains at the forefront of educational practices, adapting to meet evolving student needs. She champions an inclusive and supportive learning environment, where each student is valued, inspired, and encouraged to reach their full potential.

Beyond academics, Sabeela Ma'am is a community builder, fostering a sense of belonging among students, faculty, and parents. Her leadership extends to the continuous professional development of teachers, ensuring they are equipped with the latest pedagogical approaches.

Under her guidance, our school is not just a place of learning; it's a launchpad for future leaders. Sabeela Ma'am instills in students the values of integrity, leadership, and a sense of responsibility to society. Her unwavering dedication leaves an indelible mark on the hearts and minds of our academic community, shaping the very essence of our school's ethos.
