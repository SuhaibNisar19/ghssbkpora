#1 Bisma Ma'am

Sociology Lecturer

Bisma Fayaz is an exceptional teacher at Govt. Higher Secondary School B.K Pora, specializing in Sociology. With a wealth of experience and a passion for sociology, Bisma Ma'am has been inspiring and educating students for several years. Her dedication to her students is unparalleled.

As a sociology teacher, Bisma Ma'am imparts valuable knowledge on society, human behavior, and societal issues. She believes in nurturing critical thinking, empathy, and a deep understanding of the world we live in. Bisma Ma'am's teaching style is inclusive, ensuring that every student, regardless of their abilities, feels valued and supported. She encourages active class participation, debates, and discussions to promote a deeper understanding of sociological concepts.

Bisma Ma'am goes above and beyond, organizing extracurricular activities related to sociology, creating a vibrant community of students passionate about social sciences. Her commitment to her students' academic and personal growth makes her an invaluable asset to Govt. Higher Secondary School B.K Pora.
